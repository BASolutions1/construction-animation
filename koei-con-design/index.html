How It Works

Our Construction Process

We've refined our process over 26 years to keep you informed at every stage and deliver exactly what was agreed on.

Start Your Project →
01
Free Consultation & Estimate

We meet on-site (or via video), listen to your goals, and provide a detailed written estimate — no obligations, no pressure.

02
Design & Permitting

Our team handles drawings, City of Winnipeg permits, and any engineering sign-offs. You review and approve before we touch a shovel.

03
Construction Begins

Weekly site reports, a dedicated project manager, and our online portal keep you in the loop every step of the way.

04
Final Walkthrough & Handover

We walk the finished project with you, address any deficiencies on the spot, and hand over keys with a 2-year workmanship warranty.
